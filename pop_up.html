First register as a member of Camp David / Camp Ruth by clicking on the Register button. Fill in the application form. Then log in by using your email address and password (you have selected).

Once logged in you will see the option on you page that says: Book for an Event. Select the Event you want to register for and you will receive an email with the bank details. Once the payment have been made [with your NAME & MOBILE number as reference], the system will pick you up and you will receive another email with the logistical information of the camp.
