Faith Statement
 
 


Don’t ask what the world needs, ask what makes you COME ALIVE because the “Glory of God is men fully alive”. We are part of a larger story, it is time to activate my desires because the secrets of my life is written in my heart, take away my desires & you take out my heart.

There comes a day in a boy’s life when he first crosses the street and enters the company of great explorers with the question: Who am I? What am I made of? What am I made for? Every boy wants to be powerful, he wants to be dangerous & he wants to know: Do I have what it takes?

If a man is ever to find out whom he is and what he is here for, he has got to take that journey for himself. He’s got to get his heart back. When we go into the wilderness, into nature where we were made, we find God and meet Him in a personal relationship to restore our masculinity so that we can walk with authority. Restore me as a man so that I can walk as a son!

Camp David is a weekend breakaway into the rough country. Together a company of men journey through challenge and adventure to experience the true meaning of brotherhood, that “a friend loves at all times, but a brother is born for adversary”, he is born to stand with you through hardship and trouble.

SO, take up the Battle, fight for your heart.
