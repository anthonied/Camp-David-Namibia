Background Overview

A dream will stay a dream if you don’t wake up and do something about it. Camp David started as a dream until 5 young warriors decided to stand up & live it. Their hearts were driven into the high country, into remote places, into the untamed part of creation and they found God there.

The Lord God formed man’s body from the dust of the ground (earth)
& breathed into it the breath of life. And man became a living person.
Genesis. 2:7

The first Camp David was hosted at Umfula Game Lodge in October 2005. We
decided on a boot camp; rugged and tuff, like the army was. A military based
breakaway into nature, camping in tents, with teachings that connect you to
David, a manafter God’s own heart.

Since then its history, men get restored; they discover
true identity & get in a loving relationship
with men & GOD!
