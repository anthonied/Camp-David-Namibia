Proclaim this among the nations: Prepare war! Stir up the Mighty Men! Let all the men of war draw near, let them come up. Joel 3:9
Warriors 4 Christ is a community of men, gathering to fellowship & receives further teaching & DISSIPLESHIP. We get together every Friday morning (except school holidays) 6o’clock. After each camp it is ideal if the men who attended the camp stay in contact with each other. We don’t want a church setting just a place to be comfortable & accountable to each other; start with Worship, Testimonies and Teaching. Encourage & uplift each other let the gospel take your breath away, otherwise something else will.

What Qualities made them worthy to be David’s warriors?

  • They practice long & hard to have perfect skills.
  • They were mentally tough & determined (lion faced)

  • They were dedicated to serving God & their King – David
